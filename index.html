Project - 1

IF YOU DARE?

Typographic card game

This portfolio piece showcases 13 high-contrast cards for The Typographer's Eye game. The design employs a minimalist, deep black background with vibrant, geometric gradients (magenta, purple, and cyan) to establish a futuristic, high-energy aesthetic. The card fronts emphasize core typography, featuring large, gradient-filled letterforms (like 'A' in Helvetica) with descriptive clues. The back design uses dynamic, deconstructed display letterforms to convey a sharp, competitive tone. The overall goal is to effectively use color and form to highlight the subtle visual power of typography.

Campaign Design

For the group project माँ 2.0, we created a campaign to increase 7-Eleven's connection and relatability with the Indian public. I redesigned the logo to convey a warm, motherly convenience store—embodying care and trust for a "comfort at home" feeling. Project - 3

Package Design

Concept.

CONCEPT. is a hair grooming brand for the conscious consumer who prioritizes hair health. The packaging design reflects this commitment through themes of calmness, purity, and sustainability. The approach uses a soft, natural color palette and a minimalist, eco-friendly box design. Floral outlines symbolize the exclusive use of natural ingredients and represent growth and beauty. The carefully designed logo embodies natural flow, strength, elegance, and resilience. Ultimately, CONCEPT. aims to build a trustworthy, nature-inspired image while providing quality hair care and planning for future product expansion.
